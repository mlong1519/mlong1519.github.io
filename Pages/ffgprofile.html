Biography

From the ashes of Chicago funk-rock group, May Queen, rises Freaks for Geeks. An active tug of war between hip-hop and goodÂ old fashioned rock 'n' roll, Freaks for Geeks aims to embody two distinct musical personas. After spending the better part of two years writing and shaping their dichotomous sound, Freaks released their debut EP, Smoke 'Em If You Got 'Em in January 2013. After another two years of writing, recording, and playing venues across Chicago, Freaks for Geeks returned with their sophomore EP, Whatever It Takes. The EP, released as a limited run of individually blood-splattered cassette tapes, is a patchwork of popular culture. While referencing everything from "The Shining" and "The Invisible Man" to "Hemlock Grove" and "Dexter", Freaks uses their unique take on hip-hop fronted rock and roll to show you two sides of the same musical coin.
